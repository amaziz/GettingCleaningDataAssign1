The text file shows 180 observations and 81 variables.
Each row represents a single activity of a single subject
Example:
row 1: shows the summary of valiables associated with Subject 1 while laying 
and row 2 summarized the variable for the same subject while sitting
Overall there are 6 activities * 30 subjects and hence 180 observations (180 rows)

Row names:
subjectid: the ID number of the subject
activity: the activity label being summarized
79 variables: Each reprensets the mean of the observed activity that come from the accelerometer and gyroscope 3-axial raw signals tAcc-XYZ and tGyro-XYZ.
Each value is the mean of the activity and subject
